United School of Business Management (USBM) Admissions Admission into MBA: Admission to MBA in USBM is done through a common entrance test conducted by state govt or affiliated university or national level under merit basis which is applied both online/offline. The admission of students takes place normally in the month of June – August every year and mostly they are taken from the common counseling made through OJEE, any other tests like CAT, MAT, XAT, ATMA etc. and this is non-negotiable in all the circumstances and the individual Institutes have no role to play in the matter. The candidate has to apply online by filling an online application available at USBM official site. Candidate needs to submit the documents such as scorecard, mark sheets, passports photos, etc in the application form. After form filling is completed, the candidate needs to deposit application money through online banking /credit/debit. Admission into MCA: Admission to MCA IN USBM is done through appearing applying offline/online and having a Bachelor’s Degree Examination of a minimum of three years duration in any discipline from the any University of Odisha or from a recognized University as defined by UGC and must have passed in Mathematics at 10+2 level or at Graduate Level. Business Mathematics at +2 level are not permitted. The candidate should have obtained at least 50% (45%) in case of candidate belonging to SC/ST category

Master of Business Administration (MBA):-

Professionals who graduate with an MBA will learn business fundamentals such as leadership, communication, critical thinking and analytical skills. An MBA can enhance student’s marketability as a professional and increase the quality and quantity of job opportunities. MBA covers various facets of doing business, i.e. managing various resources namely, men, material, machines, money etc. It gives an opportunity to the students to plan, procure and manage resources in the interest of the organization. MBA equips a student with the skill to be an entrepreneur, to plan, start a venture, to contribute to the success of a social, or commercial or giant multinationals by being a part of that organization. MBA has proven to be an area of higher education to enable one to pursue one’s own area of interest, besides having decent earnings on one’s investment.

Master Of Computer Application(MCA):-

MCA is a postgraduate program with course duration of 2-years. The full form of this course is Master of Computer Application. The MCA course aims to train students in different aspects of computer programs, computer architecture, application software, operating systems, and more.Students seeking to excel in the domain of computer and information technology can take up this course.Over the years, IT industry has seen an evident growth and evolution. Due to this, more and more people are attracted and motivated to become a part of the giant IT industry. Students with a sincere inclination towards computers and technology can take up MCA course to enhance their career.
